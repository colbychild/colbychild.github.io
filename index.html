About Me

Hi, my name is Colby and I'm a GIS Specialist based in Salt Lake City, UT with 2+ years of exerience. I currently work in Utah's Public Lands Policy Coordinating Office. There I am growing my geospatial skillset and enjoy projects that require combining technical and artistic work (cartography, UI/UX, etc...). I find satisfaction in work through regularly picking up new skills, solving tricky problems, and taking pride in finished products.

I graduated from the University of Utah in 2022 with a degree in Geography focused on human-environment interaction and a degree in Philosophy of Science focused on environmental science. My studies bridged the divide between human and physical geography and I valued interdisciplinary collaboration. I believe that my unorthodox degree pairing gives me a unique and important skillset in a world that needs more integration of knowledge among specialties.

In my free time, I enjoy photography and identifying flora/fauna while out hiking, camping, or skiing.
