Emery County Arch E Print Maps

Arch E sized print maps with image samples exported at map scale to show detail. Maps made as part of a long-term project to map counties in southwest Utah. I devised a custom basemap, including a 15-layer hill shade composite, thousands of meticulously placed annotations, and fine-tuned symbology to match the map purpose to the audience.
