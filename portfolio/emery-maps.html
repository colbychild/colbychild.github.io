Emery County Arch E Print Maps

Arch E sized print maps with image samples exported at map scale to show detail. These were made as part of a long-term project to map counties in southwest Utah. I devised a custom basemap, including a 15-layer hill shade composite, thousands of annotation placements, and fine-tuned symbology to match the map purpose to the audience. County B and D class roads were emphasized as the features of interest in these maps.
